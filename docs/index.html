What is Dogmeat?

Dogmeat is your new best friend. No longer shall you need to go through a vigorously complex gui. Instead, your faithful Dogmeat takes commands.

On a literal note, Dogmeat is a WIP discord bot that is currently unavailable to the public. You may use the source to compile and modify your own bot, but Dogmeat himself will not answer to you.

What can I expect from Dogmeat?

As of right now, Dogmeat is not capable of much. Thankfully, his master is working on changing this. Regardless, you can expect:

  • Basic administrative/moderation commands including /ban and /mute
  • Offensive responses sparing no minority

If Scooty ever feels like contributing you may be able to expect more. Thanks anyway! :D
